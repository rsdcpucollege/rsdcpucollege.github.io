üìú HEBA (History, Economics, Business, Accountancy)

What is it?

A unique blend of Commerce and Humanities, designed for students who want business knowledge along with a deep understanding of society and history.

Who should choose?
  • Students aiming for Civil Services (UPSC/KAS), Law, or Teaching.
  • Those who enjoy theory, reading, and social sciences.
Why choose this?

It builds strong analytical and writing skills, essential for competitive exams and legal careers.

Future Scope:

BA, B.Com, LLB (Law), Journalism, Public Policy, Management.

üìä SEBA (Statistics, Economics, Business, Accountancy)

What is it?

A core commerce stream focused on data, numbers, and financial analysis. It replaces History with Statistics.

Who should choose?
  • Students who are good at Mathematics and logic.
  • Those aiming for professional finance degrees like CA, CS, or CMA.
Why choose this?

Statistics is the backbone of modern business. This stream offers the highest paying career options in finance.

Future Scope:

Chartered Accountant (CA), Data Analyst, Investment Banking, Actuarial Science.

üíª CEBA (Computer Science, Economics, Business, Accountancy)

What is it?

The modern commerce stream. It integrates Business Studies with Computer Science technology.

Who should choose?
  • Tech-savvy students who want to work in IT or corporate sectors.
  • Those interested in both coding/technology and business management.
Why choose this?

It opens double doors: you can enter the IT field (BCA) or the Commerce field (B.Com/BBA).

Future Scope:

BCA, B.Com, MBA in IT, Fintech, Digital Marketing, Software Management.
